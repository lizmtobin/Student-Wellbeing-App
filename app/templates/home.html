About UniSupport

UniSupport is designed to streamline access to mental health support through a centralized, user-friendly platform. The application provides students with real-time AI assistance to locate appropriate services offered by the university, and also allows students to self-refer for university counselling services and manage appointments. In addition, the Wellbeing Tracker allows users to track their moods and symptoms over time, and utilizes AI to generate personalized insights and guidance tailored to an individual's needs.

By addressing the inefficiencies of current university support structures, UniSupport has the potential to enhance student well-being, improve academic performance, and create a more supportive university experience for all.
